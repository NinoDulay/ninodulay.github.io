001 Featured Project

Shopify Discount Deal Monitor

Glenalla Group

Real-time product monitoring bot built for Glenalla Group to automatically detect discounted deals and price drops across Shopify stores. The system continuously polls product endpoints, diffs variant states using JSON comparison, and fires Slack webhook alerts the moment a deal appears — giving the team a significant purchasing advantage. Active for nearly 2 years, it directly saved the company substantial procurement costs by enabling instant deal capture that was previously missed.

Business Impact
Saved the company an estimated ₱500K+ over ~2 years in procurement costs by capturing discounted deals within seconds of appearing. Monitors 100+ products simultaneously with near-zero false positives.
